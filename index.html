About Me

Words are my world. I write to feel, to heal, and to speak the quiet stories that live inside us all. Poetry gives me wings when I need to fly, and ink helps me navigate my thoughts and dreams. I'm Colombe Bellange, a writer inspired by stillness, storms, and everything in between.

Selected Writings

  • “Letters to the Sky” — A poem about grief, hope, and unanswered prayers.
  • “My Mother’s Silence” — A reflection on generational love and strength.
  • “The Weight of Petals” — A soft rebellion told through nature’s voice.
  • “When the Moon Waits” — A short prose piece about longing and memory.

Creative Projects

I’ve contributed to local anthologies, performed spoken word at youth festivals, and led journaling workshops for young writers. My current project is a chapbook called “Whispers Between Raindrops”, coming soon.
